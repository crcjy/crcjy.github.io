I am a Ph.D. student in Mechatronic Engineering at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC).

My research began with a focus on control theory (data-driven control, model-free adaptive control, adaptive dynamic programming, model predictive control). Over time, I developed a strong interest in reinforcement learning (reinforcement learning, inverse reinforcement learning). I am currently working on these two areas, committed to applying these theories to practical scenarios such as AGVs and power systems.

Specifically, my current research topics include:

  • Safe reinforcement learning with applications to AGVs.
  • Inverse reinforcement learning and inverse optimal control from expert demonstrations.
