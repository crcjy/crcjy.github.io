I am a Ph.D. student in Mechatronic Engineering at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC).

My research began with a focus on control theory (data-driven control, model-free adaptive control, model predictive control). Over time, I developed a strong interest in adaptive dynamic programming and reinforcement learning control. I am currently working on these two areas, committed to applying these theories to practical scenarios such as AGVs and power systems.

Specifically, my current research topics include:

  • A more flexible policy-transfer based safe reinforcement learning control.
  • Safe reinforcement learning control with real-world applications.
